Nestled in the center of Detroits entertainment district at the City Club Apartments is The Statler, a French-American bistro. The Statler offers classic French-influenced cuisine with a slightly modern twist, catering to both adventurous and traditional diners alike. A chef-driven concept, The Statler is an approachable bistro modeled after Parisian neighborhood cafes. We take pride in using ingredients sourced from both local and international purveyors. Our menu was created by Certified Master Chef Daniel Scannell and Executive Chef Sean Force. When visiting The Statler, you are surrounded by sweeping views of downtown Detroit. You are within walking distance from venues like Comerica Park, The DAC, Detroit Opera House, Fox Theatre, Little Caesars Arena, and more. You are likely to bump into friends and find yourself in great conversations with neighboring tables.

Valet parking is available Thursday - Sunday.

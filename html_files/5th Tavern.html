5th Tavern is an upscale casual dining restaurant featuring American style cuisine prepared from scratch. Our menu features everything from hand cut steaks and seafood to pasta, sandwiches, burgers and more. Paired with our professional well trained staff and a beautiful unique ambiance. We offer 12 taps, of mainly Michigan craft beers along with a large assortment of craft beers by the bottle. Our premium bar also features an assortment of high end wines and top shelf liquors prepared by our energetic bar staff.

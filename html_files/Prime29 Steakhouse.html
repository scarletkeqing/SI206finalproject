Prime29 - West Bloomfield
Contemporary, Casual-Fine Dining. At Prime29 Steakhouse we serve only the best cuts of prime beef, aged 29 days and fresh seafood together with exemplary wines and top shelf martinis and cocktails. We also feature market fresh vegetarian & gluten free chef's compositions. At Prime29 Steakhouse we are passionate about delivering a world class dining experience with striking dinner and dessert compositions, exceptional tableside service, and exclusive drink pairings from start to finish.

Prime29 Steakhouse, located at the corner of Orchard Lake Rd and Maple also features a gorgeous outdoor covered patio showcasing a 10ft, 360 degree fireplace; a spacious lounge, and 3 beautiful dining rooms perfect for parties up to 200.
